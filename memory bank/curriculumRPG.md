- **Shadow Training**: Hints allowed, 0.5x XP reward. Focus on L1/L2 Cognitive levels.
- **Guardian Skirmish**: Standard TKA Difficulty, 1x XP reward. Focus on L2 Cognitive level.
- **The Time Rift**: Mixed questions from current & previous sub-chapters, 1.5x XP reward. Focus on L3 Cognitive level.
- **Ancient Dragon Simulation**: Full exam simulation. 100% official TKA tone.
- **Energy (Hearts)**: 
    - Max: 5 Hearts.
    - Loss: -1 Heart per failed Battle (HP 0).
    - Recovery: Wait (30 mins/heart), "Share to WA" task, or "Watch Ad" (Rewarded Video).
- **The Mentor Guild (Marketplace)**: 
    - Hire verified teachers for 1-on-1 sessions.
    - Native "Recommended Mentor" ads triggered after 3x battle failures.
- **Premium Items**: "Sage Scrolls" (unlocks advanced stages) and "Elixirs" (instant energy) purchased with Gold/Coins.
